About Us

OMS Beverages is conceptualized as an automated beverage manufacturing plant based in Ikorodu, Lagos Nigeria, encompassing capacities for end-to-end processing and packaging of beverages from varied forms of water to juice drinks and ready-to-drink-tea (RTD). Our Vision

The vision of the company is to enrich the lives of the people of Nigeria and its environs through the provision of accessible safe drinking water and nutritional fruit drinks; while also delivering healthy returns on investments to the investors
