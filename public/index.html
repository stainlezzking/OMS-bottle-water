About US

OMS Beverages is conceptualized as an automated beverage manufacturing plant based in Ikorodu, Lagos Nigeria, encompassing capacities for end-to-end processing and packaging of beverages from varied forms of water to juice drinks and ready-to-drink-tea (RTD).
